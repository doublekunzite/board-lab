POGO

Swinging a leg or arm like a counterweight to generate momentum or improve body position for a deadpoint.

Pogo on "Larry Wheels"

Sideways
Hover to play

Swinging the left leg to pogo on "Larry Wheels" V10 / 7c+ @ 60.

Technique Notes

A pogo helps you generate momentum for a deadpoint. This normally requires pretty good holds that enable you to swing your body around. Throwing your arm or leg towards the next hold can help improve your body position for catching it.
